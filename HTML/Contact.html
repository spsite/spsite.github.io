Frequently Asked Questions

•How can I track my order?

-Once your order has shipped, we will send you a shipping confirmation email with a link to track the order on the carrier's website. By clicking the link, you will be able to check the status of your order once it's in transit.

•How do I prepare the Juice?

-Our system is fast and easy to use. 1 gallon pre-measured mix pitchers available to assure cost control and flavor consistency. Prepare all flavors using seven parts water and one part juice concentrate.

•Do I need to refrigerate the juice concentrate?

-You don’t need to refrigerate the concentrate before or after opening it. You only need to refrigerate the juice after it has been prepared. We do not recommend storing a prepared mix for more than 24 hours.

•Are Penny Juice concentrates from real fruit?

-All of Penny Juice fruit juice concentrates are 100% real fruit juice and have no added sugars. You can feel confident knowing your children are consuming natural products
