Our Story

Penny Juice is a family-owned business that was founded in 2001 and we have over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry. We handle all aspects of the juice making process beginning with the selection of ingredients to packing the juice to ship to our customers. We take pride in being able to have full transparency with our customers about the journey that our product has, from the fresh fruit to the customerâ€™s door.

Our Mission

We began this company with the goal of making practical but healthy juices concentrates that all children could enjoy. We have 15 delicious flavors available so that every child can choose their favorite. Using and storing the juice concentrates is simple in order to help with high pace environments where the attention must be placed on children first. Penny Juice products do not need to be refrigerated before or after opening, only until the product has been prepared to serve, making the process worry free.

Penny Juice Benefits

Penny Juice products comply with all requirements for CACFP, subsidy, and nutrition set forth by the FDA, and have added vitamin C to help boost the immune system. There are no added sugars or artificial flavoring in any of our products. While do offer flavors with color added, none are artificial, but we understand some people are worried about colorants so we offer color free options too. Consuming fruit juice makes children happy and we keep the juice that they drink healthy.
